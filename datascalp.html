Flight Picker

Descripion

An online platform that allows consumers to report poor consumer experiences, converting those inputs into an online consumer dashboard that visually ranks companies, thus empowering consumers with the ability to make better purchasing decisions and drive change by impacting consumer demand.

My Role

As the full stack developer for the project, I performed several key roles. First and foremost, I was responsible for designing the database models, ensuring that the data would be properly structured and easily accessible for the rest of the application.

In addition to database design, I also took on the task of converting Figma designs into screens, ensuring that the user interface matched the designer's vision while also adhering to best practices for user experience. This included creating responsive layouts that would work across a variety of screen sizes and devices.

Once the application was complete, I also took on the responsibility of deploying it to AWS, ensuring that the system was configured properly and all dependencies were in place.

Finally, I added Google Analytics to the application, allowing us to track usage and gain valuable insights into user behavior. Throughout the project, I worked closely with the rest of the team to ensure that everything was running smoothly and that the project was delivered on time and to a high standard.
